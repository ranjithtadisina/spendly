Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Spendly ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and stores information when you use our personal expense tracking service. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Data We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense records — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps and page visits.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expense history and summaries.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To diagnose errors and improve app performance.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secured database. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This causes your browser to make a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address per Google's own privacy policy. No other third-party analytics or advertising services are used.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or want to request deletion of your data, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.
